Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Cocoa powder
  • Cooking chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla essence
  • Vegetable oil

Recipe Overview

  1. Preheat the oven to 170Â°C.
  2. Heat up 1/4 cup of oil in a pan.
  3. When the oil is hot enough, put in 85g of cooking chocolate then mix them up.
  4. Let the chocolate-oil mixture to cool down.
  5. Sift and combine the following ingredients in a separate bowl:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Once the chocolate mixture in step 1 has cooled down, mix in the following ingredients:
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 cup sugar (up to preference)
    • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  7. Combine ingredients in step 5 and 6 thoroughly.
  8. Add any additional toppings if preferred.
  9. Oil up your baking tray, pour in the brownies batter, then put it inside the oven.
  10. Bake for minimum 20 minutes, or until the inside is no longer watery.
  11. Allow the brownies to cool for 30 minutes after baking.
  12. Enjoy your freshly-baked brownies with your loved ones.
